About the App

GündemTR is a news application that provides users with access to current news from Türkiye and around the world. The app is designed to help users follow trending and recent news in a simple and accessible way.

Content Source Information

The news content displayed in GündemTR is obtained through third-party news API services. The app does not create original news articles. It presents news content from external sources to users.

Publisher / Source Information

Each article shown in the app is associated with its relevant source or publisher information whenever provided by the content provider. Source and publisher details are displayed to users together with the news content.

Editorial Responsibility

GündemTR does not modify, rewrite, or edit the original news content received from third-party providers. The application acts as a news aggregator and displays the content as supplied by those providers.
