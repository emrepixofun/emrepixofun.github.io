Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application "Nöbetçi Eczane" accesses, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

The app helps users view publicly available on-duty pharmacy information for selected cities. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or health monitoring services.

1. Information We Access or Collect

The app may access or process the following categories of data:

  • Selected city information (for example, Istanbul, Ankara, or Izmir) to show the relevant on-duty pharmacies.
  • Approximate location-related input only when you manually choose a city inside the app. The app does not collect precise GPS location unless separately implemented and disclosed.
  • Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, crash data, and basic diagnostic information.
  • IP address and request metadata that may be processed by the API provider, hosting provider, or Firebase infrastructure when requests are made.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data when ads are shown through Google AdMob.
  • Usage data, such as general interaction with screens and features, for app functionality, diagnostics, and service improvement.

2. Health Data Disclosure

Because this app displays pharmacy-related information, Google Play may treat some app content as health-related. For transparency:

  • The app displays publicly available pharmacy information, including pharmacy name, address, phone number, district, and map location.
  • The app may process the user-selected city in order to show the relevant pharmacy list for that area.
  • This city selection is used only to provide the requested pharmacy results and app functionality.
  • The app does not collect, store, or share personal medical records, prescriptions, diagnosis information, treatment history, symptoms, insurance data, or any other personal health data provided by the user.
  • The app does not create a health profile about the user.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To show on-duty pharmacies for the selected city
  • To retrieve and display address, phone, and map information
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • To cache pharmacy data temporarily for faster loading and reduced repeated requests
  • To display advertisements through supported ad services
  • To detect errors, abuse, or technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that process certain data as part of their infrastructure:

Google AdMob

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction and performance data

More information: Google Ads Policy

Firebase Realtime Database

  • Temporarily cached pharmacy results
  • Technical request data processed by Google infrastructure

External Pharmacy Data API Providers

  • Selected city parameter sent to retrieve the relevant pharmacy list
  • Network metadata such as IP address or technical request headers may be processed by the provider

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Pharmacy listing data may be cached temporarily in Firebase Realtime Database to improve speed and reduce repeated API calls.
  • Cached pharmacy data is generally retained for a limited period, typically less than 24 hours, depending on app logic.
  • The app is not designed to permanently store user personal profiles or personal health records.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only as needed with service providers that support app functionality, such as advertising, hosting, database, analytics, or API infrastructure providers.
